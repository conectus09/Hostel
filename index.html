Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes LuxeHostel the Best Hostel 2025?

LuxeHostel won the "Best Hostel 2025" award for our exceptional combination of luxury amenities at affordable prices, 50+ premium services, vibrant community of 1200+ students from 30+ countries, world-class facilities, and outstanding customer service. Our commitment to excellence, student safety, and holistic development sets us apart from other hostels.

Can students from different countries live together?

Absolutely! One of LuxeHostel's greatest strengths is our diverse international community. We have students from 30+ countries including USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, France, UAE, Singapore, Japan, and many more. This cultural diversity enriches the hostel experience and helps students develop global perspectives. We actively organize international events and cultural exchanges.

What is included in the rent?

Everything is included! Your rent covers: furnished room with AC/furniture, all utilities (electricity, water, WiFi), 3x daily gourmet meals, daily housekeeping, laundry service, 24/7 security, fitness center access, and access to all 50+ services including gaming zone, study hub, rooftop lounge, medical support, and more. No hidden charges whatsoever.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI/PhonePay payments through secure QR codes for instant transactions. For demo bookings, you'll see a demo QR code during checkout. You have 5 minutes to complete the payment. Payments are 100% secure with encryption. After payment, you'll receive instant confirmation and booking details via email.

How is the booking process?

Our booking process is simple and secure: 1) Click "Book Now" and fill in your details, 2) Select your room type and check-in date, 3) Choose your duration, 4) Review the price breakdown, 5) Select UPI payment, 6) Scan the QR code and complete payment within 5 minutes, 7) Receive instant confirmation. Your room is reserved immediately after successful payment.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, we offer flexible cancellation policies. Cancel 30 days before check-in: full refund. Cancel 15-30 days before: 50% refund of security deposit. Cancel less than 15 days: security deposit forfeited. We understand that plans change, so we're flexible while protecting our operations. Contact our support team for any cancellation requests.

What are the check-in procedures?

Check-in is flexible! You can arrive anytime on your check-in date. Our staff will assist with room allocation based on your preferences, WiFi setup, orientation tour, and issuing your biometric access card. If you're arriving late, please inform us in advance. We also offer complimentary pickup from the metro station/airport for first-time arrivals.

Are guests and visitors allowed?

Yes, guests are welcome! Daytime visitors (9 AM - 9 PM) can visit anytime with prior notification. Overnight guests require management approval and must check-in at the reception desk. We maintain this policy to ensure everyone's safety and comfort. Parents, relatives, and friends are always welcome during visiting hours.

What security measures are in place?

LuxeHostel prioritizes your safety with: 24/7 CCTV surveillance in all common areas, biometric locks on individual rooms, trained security staff on duty round-the-clock, emergency panic buttons in all rooms, secure gated entrance, restricted vehicle access, periodic security audits, and immediate incident response protocols. We also conduct regular safety drills and orientation sessions.

Is parking available?

Yes! We offer free secure parking for motorcycles, scooters, and bicycles in our dedicated parking area with 24/7 surveillance. Car parking is available at reasonable additional charges depending on vehicle type and duration. All vehicles must be registered at reception for security purposes. We ensure safe and secure parking for all residents' vehicles.
